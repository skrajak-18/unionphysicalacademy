Frequently Asked Questions

Academy join karne se pehle ke aam sawalon ke seedhe aur sateek jawab yahan padhiye.

Kya naye students ko shuruat se running sikhayi jaati hai?

Haan, bilkul! Jo bilkul beginners hain unhe step-by-step pacing, breathing patterns aur stamina loading techniques ke sath running shuru karayi jaati hai taaki unhe koi internal injury na ho.

High Jump ke liye kaun si techniques sikhayi jaati hain?

Hum Bihar Police aur Daroga ke liye trending 'Scissor Cut' aur scientific 'Tiger Jump' dono systems ki advanced technical practices expert trainers ke supervision me sand-beds par karate hain.

Kya hostel aur rehane ki suvidha bhi upalabdh hai?

Basuara (Darbhanga) ke bahar se aane wale candidates ke liye hum nearby standard hostel tie-ups aur proper strategic military nutrition food plans suggest karne me poori sahayata karte hain.

Mock tests kis din conduct karaye jate hain?

Har Sunday ko subah exam-like mahol me strict mock tests hote hain jisme time aur measurement parameters perfectly record kiye jate hain aur scorecard distribute hota hai.
